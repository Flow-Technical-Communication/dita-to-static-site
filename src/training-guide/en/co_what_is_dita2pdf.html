What is DITA2PDF?

DITA2PDF is an alternative method of transforming your DITA files into PDF files. While it is possible to do so through use of the DITA OT, the DITA2PDF method ensures the end-result is a high-quality PDF file with the appropriate lay-out for your product/client.

DITA2PDF is done by importing your DITA files into an intermediary program such as Adobe FrameMaker, through use of a plugin. FrameMaker then allows you to apply the template of your choice to your documentation. Unlike the Open Toolkit, intermediary programs are much more user-friendly to work with and allow you to customize your templates. As such, you end up with a high-quality PDF that you can adjust where necessary.
