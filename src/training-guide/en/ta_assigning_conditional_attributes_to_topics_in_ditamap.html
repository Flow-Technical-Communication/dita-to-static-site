To assign conditions to an entire topic

  1. Open your DITA map in your Editor area.
  2. Select the topic you want to apply a conditional processing attribute to.
  3. In the Attributes list, click in the empty field next to a conditional processing attribute and enter a value.
  4. Press Enter.
    The attribute and its value are now applied to the whole topic and all its contents.
    Note: Although this method is not recommended, you can also apply the condition within the topic itself by opening it in the Editor area and applying it to the <topic> element in the same way as when you assign conditions to a part of a topic.
Make sure any links to this topic are marked with a conditional processing attribute as well:
  • For inline links, see To assign conditions to a part of a topic.
  • For links in a relationship table, see To assign conditions to a topic reference.
