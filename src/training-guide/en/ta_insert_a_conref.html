To insert a conref via the menu

  1. Position your cursor where you want the conref to appear.
  2. Choose DITA > Reuse Content.
  3. In the Reuse Content window, navigate to your warehouse file, for example warehouse_steps.dita.
  4. Select the correct element from the list.
    Tip: Use the Show elements of type drop-down list to narrow down the list of elements.
  5. Click Insert and close.
