To generate a book from a DITA map

In order to create a bookfile according to your specified template and requirements, make sure you have a destination folder ready which contains the correct ditafmx-bookbuild.ini and DITAVAL files.
  1. In Adobe FrameMaker, choose DITA-FMx > Generate Book from Map.
  2. Navigate to your DITA map and click Save.
  3. In Specify a bookfile name, specify a name and location for your bookfile and click Save.
    FrameMaker starts to generate a bookfile; this may take a few minutes. When it is done, it also saves a logfile in the folder you specified, which details the transformation process and any errors (if applicable).
