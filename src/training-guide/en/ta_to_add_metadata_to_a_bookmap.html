To add metadata to a DITA bookmap

Unlike in a regular DITA map, metadata is added through use of a <bookmeta> element, rather than a <topicmeta> element.

  1. Open your bookmap in the Editor area.
  2. Underneath your title, but still within the <bookmap> element, add the element <bookmeta>.
  3. Within <bookmeta>, add an element <metadata>.
  4. Press Enter to add your metadata, such as author, bookrights and category.
