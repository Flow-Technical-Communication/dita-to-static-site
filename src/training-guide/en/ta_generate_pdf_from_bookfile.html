To generate a PDF from a bookfile

  1. Open your bookfile in FrameMaker.
  2. Choose File > Save as PDF.
  3. In the Save Book window, specify a name and a location for your PDF and click Save.
  4. In the PDF setup for Selected Files, specify the settings you want and click Set.
    FrameMaker now creates a PDF according to your specifications.
