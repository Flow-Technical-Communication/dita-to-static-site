To delete rows and columns

  1. Place your mouse pointer above the column or next to the row that you want to delete.
    Your mouse pointer turns into an arrow and the column or row is highlighted in blue.
  2. Do one of the following:
    1. Right-click and select Table > Delete Column(s) or Table > Delete Row(s).
    2. Select Delete the selected table row(s). or Delete the selected table column(s). in the Main toolbar.
